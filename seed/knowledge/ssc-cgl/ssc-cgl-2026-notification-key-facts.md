# SSC CGL 2026 — Notification key facts

> Provenance: Shishya-curated summary of the SSC Combined Graduate Level
> Examination 2026 notification published by the Staff Selection Commission
> at https://ssc.gov.in/ on 21 May 2026. Cross-verified against multiple
> independent secondary publishers on 12 June 2026. The official notification
> PDF on ssc.gov.in is the canonical source — always direct students there
> for legally binding details.

## Important dates

- Notification released: 21 May 2026
- Online application window: 21 May 2026 to 22 June 2026 (closes 11:00 PM)
- Last date to pay application fee: 23 June 2026 (11:00 PM)
- Tier 1 (CBT) exam: August–September 2026 (tentative)
- Tier 2 (CBT) exam: December 2026 (tentative)

## Vacancies

- Total vacancies announced: 12,256 (tentative; SSC revises post-wise counts
  during the cycle)
- Spread across approximately 40 Group B and Group C posts in central
  government ministries, departments, and organisations.

## Application fee

- Fee: ₹100 (one hundred rupees)
- Fully exempted: women candidates, and candidates belonging to SC, ST,
  PwBD (persons with benchmark disabilities), and ESM (ex-servicemen).
- Payment is online via the SSC portal (BHIM UPI, net banking, or cards).

## Eligibility — education

- Essential qualification for most posts: Bachelor's degree in any
  discipline from a recognised university, completed on or before the
  cut-off date stated in the notification.
- Junior Statistical Officer (JSO): Bachelor's degree with at least 60% in
  Mathematics in Class 12, OR Bachelor's degree with Statistics as a subject.
- Statistical Investigator Grade-II: Bachelor's degree with Statistics as a
  compulsory or elective subject.
- Final-year students can apply if they can produce proof of having passed
  by the cut-off date in the notification.

## Eligibility — age (as of 1 August 2026)

- Most Group C posts: 18–27 years
- Most Group B posts: 18–30 years
- JSO / Statistical Investigator and a few other posts: up to 32 years
- Exact per-post age bands are listed in the official notification's
  post table; the bands differ by post, so students must check their
  target post specifically.

## Age relaxations (over the upper limit)

- SC / ST: +5 years
- OBC (non-creamy layer): +3 years
- PwBD (unreserved): +10 years; PwBD + OBC: +13 years; PwBD + SC/ST: +15 years
- Ex-servicemen: 3 years after deduction of military service from actual age
- Other special categories (e.g. J&K domicile during specified period,
  widows/divorced women for some posts) carry relaxations listed in the
  official notification.

## How to apply

1. Register once on the SSC portal (ssc.gov.in) using One-Time Registration
   (OTR) — Aadhaar-linked registration speeds up later steps.
2. Log in, choose "Combined Graduate Level Examination 2026", fill the form,
   upload photo/signature per the size specs in the notification.
3. Pay the fee (if applicable) before the fee deadline.
4. A correction window of a few days opens after the application closes;
   only limited fields can be edited and a correction fee applies.

## Common pitfalls

- The application deadline (22 June 2026) and the fee deadline (23 June
  2026) are different days — paying on the last day after failing to submit
  the form does not help.
- Photo/signature uploads that violate the size or background specs are the
  most common rejection reason — follow the notification's annexure exactly.
- The age cut-off date (1 August 2026) is not the application date —
  calculate age as of the cut-off date.
